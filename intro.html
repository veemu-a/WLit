PHOTOGRAPHY



PHOTOGRAPHY, this word moves me!!! It brings out the best of me. I've not always been fond of photography since the beginning, in fact, I never realized that I was capable of doing it. Photography has become a part of me, I believe my photography defines me as a person. My passion for photography is growing as my portfolio of photos.

I'm a self-learned photographer who believes a perfect photograph doesn't exist it's you who make it effortlessly amazing. Photography is very vague in itself, the more you dig in deep more you come across mind-boggling ideas. I believe the skills in photography cannot be taught sitting in class and jotting down all the points in a notebook. I'm a firm believer that photography is about visualization and stories that you can portray through a photo. I've been doing photography for 2 years and still exploring and learning but most importantly I'm relishing doing it.
